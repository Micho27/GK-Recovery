Train harder and recover faster with the Recoverite Compression Tights Starter Kit, combining the performance and healing benefits of compression and ice. Compression tights are used by amateurs and professionals alike to help prevent muscle soreness and fatigue, lower risk of injury, lower perceived exertion, promote blood flow and improve oxygenation of muscle tissue while working out. Post workout, compression active-wear also help to speed up recovery times. Featuring expertly designed SuperTube 1.0’ compression technology, these tights combine state of the art compression technology with innovative ‘Ice-otonic Pocket System 1.0’ for optimal ice-compression recovery.

No more fumbling with bulky ice blocks or frozen bags of peas, these stable, breathable, and durable pockets keep the included slimline ClayFlex™ mouldable gel ice packs securely in place for pain relief even on the move. The ClayFlex™ gel composition is also scientifically designed for dual use heat application, which means the recovery system can be used for hot and cold therapy.

Recoverite Compression Tights are constructed using state of the art manufacturing technology, the highest quality fabrics, and ultra comfortable flat stitch to ensure high grade, high performance compression.
